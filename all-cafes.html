How to Use

  1. Add cafés you like using the + Add Café button.
  2. Click Shuffle to get a random suggestion from your list.
  3. Use the eye button to temporarily hide a café from the shuffle.
  4. Edit or delete cafés anytime from your list.
  5. All your data is saved in your browser (no account needed).
